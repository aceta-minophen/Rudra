A smart healthcare assistant robot
intended for home and hospital use.

We have an idea that we
want to bring to life
for that, we need your help

following and tracking

Rudra is intended to follow the designated human in-toe and always be en garde for obstacles that the human might run into. This also gives Rudra the ability to assist the visually impaired and helps them navigate their surroundings safely without the need for anyone else.

Fitness Tracking

As well as capturing fitness data from smartwatch, Rudra will alert the user to drink water on a regular basis(ensure that water is drunk by logging activity using camera). It will also remind the user of specific medicing timings and food intake instructions.

Voice assistant

A key feature of Rudra is to be a constant companion in sickness and in health. Talking and cheering up humans is what Rudra is built for.

Companion App

One app to rule them all. Rudra Companion App will be extremely crucial in the initial setup of the robot. Then, most of the data transfer will also be accomplished using this app.

Teams

Contributers of Rudra are divided into the following teams:
